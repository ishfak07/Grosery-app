Privacy policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

This policy explains how Puttalam Drop handles information when you use our grocery ordering and delivery application.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as name, mobile number, and delivery address.
  • Order details, shopping lists, receipts, payment method, and status.
  • Images you choose from your camera or gallery for orders or support.
  • Support messages, delivery reviews, and account requests.
  • Device notification tokens needed to send order updates.

The app does not sell personal data. Camera and photo access is used only when you choose to attach an image.

How we use information

We use the information to create and deliver orders, contact you about an order, provide customer support, prevent misuse, maintain business records, and send service notifications.

Service providers

Google Firebase provides authentication, database, file storage, notifications, server functions, and website hosting. Images uploaded by older app versions may remain in Cloudinary until they are removed through the legacy-media cleanup process. These providers process data only to operate the app and its infrastructure.

Retention and deletion

You can delete your account from Profile in the app. You can also use our web deletion request page. Active orders must first be completed or cancelled.

On deletion, we remove your authentication account, profile, support conversations, notifications, password-reset requests, and personal images held in Firebase Storage. Closed order and accounting records may be retained for legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, and dispute purposes for up to seven years after account deletion, but they are anonymized and disconnected from your account. Completed or rejected web deletion request records are removed after 90 days. Older Cloudinary image references are placed in a restricted cleanup queue and removed from customer and order records.

Security and choices

Access is controlled by authenticated roles and server-side security rules. No internet service is completely risk-free, but we limit access to the information needed for each customer, administrator, or assigned delivery worker.

You can update your name and address in Profile, disable notifications in device settings, or contact support inside the app about privacy, correction, or deletion.

Children

Puttalam Drop is a general grocery service and is not directed to children under 13. A parent or guardian should manage any household order placed by a minor.

Contact

Use the Support area in the Puttalam Drop app. For account deletion when you cannot access the app, submit the secure web request form.
