Delete your account

Delete inside the app

The quickest method is Profile > Delete account. Confirm your password and the app will remove the account if it has no active order.

Request deletion from the web

Use this form if you cannot sign in. We will verify that you control the account before processing the request. Submitting the form does not immediately delete an active order.

What is deleted

Authentication, profile information, support conversations, notifications, reset requests, and personal Firebase Storage images are deleted. Closed order and accounting records may be retained in anonymized form for legal and business-record purposes. See the privacy policy for full details.
